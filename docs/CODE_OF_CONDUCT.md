# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful. Disagreements about submissions, App ID disputes, and policy
decisions are normal — keep them focused on the issue, not the person.

## Not acceptable

- Harassment, personal attacks, or insulting language directed at maintainers,
  contributors, or other users
- Repeatedly reopening or re-litigating a decision maintainers have already made
  and explained
- Spamming issues, pull requests, or discussions
- Doxxing or sharing someone else's private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, lock discussions, or block users from
interacting with this repository and other `flatforge-hub` repositories at their
discretion. As with submission decisions (see
[POLICY.md — Editorial discretion](POLICY.md#editorial-discretion)), this may
happen without prior warning or a detailed explanation, particularly in cases of
spam, harassment, or coordinated abuse.

A block is not necessarily permanent and may be reconsidered if circumstances change.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct, report
it to **evtcsuha5@mozmail.com**.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to this repository, all per-app repositories under
the `flatforge-hub` organization, and any official Flatforge communication channels.
